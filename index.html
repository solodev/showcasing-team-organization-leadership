Meet the Chief Astronaut

Yuri Garfield is a cosmonaut who became the first cat to journey unto outer space, achieving a major accomplishment in the LunarXP exploration. As a kitten, he was always into flying and exploration. After serving in Air Force, he joined the LunarXP space exploration. His ship Valkyrie-1 landed on the moon successfully and delivered enough supplies such as cat food and cat litter to start a cat colony on Mars.
